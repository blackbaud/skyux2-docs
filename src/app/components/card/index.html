The card module creates a small container to highlight important information. Multiple cards are generally grouped together to display information about related items, and each card can include three optional parts: a title, body content, and an action.

Creates a a small container to highlight important information. Specifies an action that users can perform on the card. Specifies the content to display in the body of the card. Specifies a title to identify what the card represents. Indicates whether to display a checkbox to the right of the card title. Users can select multiple checkboxes and perform actions on the selected cards. This property accepts boolean values. Indicates whether the card is selected. Cards can handle a variety of scenarios, but multiple cards are generally grouped together to display information about related items. For example, cards can present a stack or carousel of to-do items or a matrix of images.

Cards frequently present users with a call to action. They can use visual cues to alert users about the need for action, and they can also include actions directly on the cards.
