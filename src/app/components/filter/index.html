Inline Filters
Inline filters

Use inline filters to apply up to three simple filters to a list or tile. This pattern works well with checkboxes or HTML select fields, but not with complex selection controls.

Modal filters

Use modal filters:

  • To apply four or more simple filters to a list or tile.
  • To apply complex selection controls.

Present filters in a modal or a sectioned modal depending on the quantity and nature of the filters.
