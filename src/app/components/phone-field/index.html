The phone field module creates a field and dropdown to validate international phone numbers. The phone field input directive validates the phone number that users enter in the field against standards for the country that they select in the dropdown. For validation, phone numbers are driven through the ngModel attribute that you specify on an input element or a FormControl in a reactive form.
